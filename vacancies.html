Job Specification – Web Developer

To work as a Web Developer for Hotbeans you will need some Background knowledge and training. Listed below are desired qualifications to be able to work for Hotbeans.

GCSE in IT (or BTEC equivalent) GCSE in Art (or BTEC equivalent) Level 2 award in Creating a Website Level 2 Certificate in Interactive Media Level 3 Diploma in IT User Skills Level 4 in Maths/English Minimum Responsibilities – Web Developer

By being a Web Developer for Hotbeans, you will have many responsibilities linked to your authority. This is to ensure our high standards are met and everything runs super smoothly. Listed below are a few responsibilities linked to you.

Designing, building, testing and possibly maintaining a website or software application Working with teams to resolve any conflicts, develop content criteria, prioritize requirements and find and agree o solutions Performing and directing any website updates Consistently backing up files Making sure codes are met with company standard

Job Specification – IT Technician

To work as a IT Technician for Hotbeans you will need some Background knowledge and training. Listed below are desired qualifications to be able to work for Hotbeans.

Level 3 in IT or BTEC 1 Years Experience in IT Understand Cyber Security Understand Data Protection Good Communication Skills Level 4 in Maths/English Minimum Responsibilities – IT Technician

By being a IT Technician for Hotbeans, you will have many responsibilities linked to your authority. This is to ensure our high standards are met and everything runs super smoothly. Listed below are a few responsibilities linked to you.

Familiarity with supporting all common end user software including Microsoft Office. Responding to calls in a proactive manner. Work with clients making sure systems are functional and secure. Team Player Confident articulate Excellent telephone manner 1st Line Support experience
Job Specification – Junior Developer

To work as a Junior Developer for Hotbeans you will need to be finishing a web Development course or be leading on from a Course. This is so we can further your knowledge and potential give you a job.

Level 4 in Maths/English Minimum Responsibilities – Junior Developer

By being a Junior Developer for Hotbeans, you will have many responsibilities linked to your authority. This is to ensure our high standards are met and everything runs super smoothly. Listed below are a few responsibilities linked to you.

• Good understanding of content management systems. • Good understanding of search engine optimization principles. • Proficient understanding of cross-browser compatibility issues. • Excellent visual design skills. • Up-to-date experience with international web protocols, standards, and technologies. • Creative and open to new ideas. • Adaptable and willing to learn new techniques. • Excellent communication skills.
